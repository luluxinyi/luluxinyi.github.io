Survey

HappySignals Survey is a tool that can be used to get feedback from employees. The employee will get an email after the support session, and after rating the support directly from the email, they'll be redirected to give additional details about the case. Provided information will give companies actionable information about their support services.

We designed the email templates and the user interface to be easily branded for the customer companies.
